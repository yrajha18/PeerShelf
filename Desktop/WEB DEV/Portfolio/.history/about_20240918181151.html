My Journey

My journey into the world of programming began with curiosity—curiosity about how lines of text could transform into powerful applications, how algorithms could solve real-world problems, and how creativity could thrive in the digital realm.

As I delved deeper, I discovered my love for AI and ML—the way they empower machines to learn, adapt, and make decisions. Whether it’s training neural networks, exploring natural language processing, or diving into computer vision, I’m constantly amazed by the possibilities.

What Drives Me

What keeps me going? It’s the thrill of turning ideas into elegant solutions, the joy of collaborating with fellow tech enthusiasts, and the satisfaction of creating something that impacts lives—even if it’s just a few lines of code.
